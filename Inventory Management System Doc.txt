**Models**
- User
	- name (string,req)
	- company name (string,req)
	- email (string, req, email validator - mongodb)
	- password (string, req)
	- otp (string)
- Category
	- Category Name (string, req)
	- user_id (id, req)
- Product
	- Product Name (String, req)
	- Quantity (Integer, req)
	- Price (Float, req)
	- Category_id  (id, req)
	- user_id  (id, req)
